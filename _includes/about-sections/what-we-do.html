NOVERRA CAPITAL

Noverra Capital is our investment company, which focuses on acquiring new companies for our group. We invest the earned cash-flows from our operating companies to provide the capital required for acquisitions without outside passive investors, enabling flexibility and a very long investment horizon.

NOVERRA SUPPORT SERVICES

Noverra Support Services provides strategic support for each of our operating businesses enabling the leadership teams to develop and execute effective strategies for continued success of the business. We have found that many companies have strong sales and operational management, but that many business owners who wish to transition out of their companies have been solely responsible for the financial and strategic elements of the business. Noverra Support Services has been developed to enable transitions for exiting owners which may otherwise be challenging due to the strategic gap left in successful companies when the owner retires.

NOVERRA LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT

Noverra Leadership Development delivers and deploys leadership development content and shares best practices from across our group, to our operating company executives, managers and staff, enabling us to proactively develop the capabilities of the teams that successfully lead our group companies. We have a bias towards developing the existing teams as opposed to bringing in new management teams. The capacity of our leaders, managers and staff in each of our operating companies is the constraint for our growth and continued success; our leadership development programs enable us to continuously grow this capacity.
