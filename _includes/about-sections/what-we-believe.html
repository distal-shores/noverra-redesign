The personal values of the three Owners of the Noverra Group of Companies drive the business strategy and decision making of our group. These values, which may seem old fashioned, are central in our strategic approach and the manner in which we conduct business:

1. INTEGRITY, HONESTY, CLARITY

We believe in, and practice, the model of business where we say what we mean; we try to be as clear and honest as possible; and, we always honor our word. If we shake someoneâ€™s hand, it is as good as a signature. There are lots of opportunities in business and in life, and we have found it to be most rewarding when we do business with people that share this mindset and practice.

2. STRONG, TRUSTED RELATIONSHIPS

We show respect and fairness in all of our dealings. We strive to build strong, long, trusting relationships with everyone we do business with. We believe that the strength of relationships with our people, customers, suppliers and everyone we do business with, will be the key to our value over the long run. Having wonderful relationships with the people you do business with also just makes things more fun.

3. PRUDENT, STRATEGIC, LONG-TERM FOCUS

We aim to build a wonderful group of companies for the long term, which we think of as 25 years or more. We have no interest in selling our companies; we have a desire to strategically invest over the very long run, and a naturally prudent mindset in our investment approach.

4. COLLABORATIVE INDEPENDENCE

We believe that each business in our group should operate independent of one another. We believe that local companies, led by local leaders produce the best results driven by accountability and clarity of focus. Collaborating across the group to share great practices and tools enables the benefits of being a part of a group of businesses without any of the constraints and friction of integration

5. STRONG LEADERS BUILD GREAT COMPANIES

We believe that ultimately the strength of leadership throughout our companies will drive the success of our businesses over the long run, and as such, we proactively invest in the development of the leaders within each of our businesses. Building great leaders from the President on down to each staff member in our companies is a key focus of our efforts, and we believe its is the only way to create long term sustainable success in any organization.
