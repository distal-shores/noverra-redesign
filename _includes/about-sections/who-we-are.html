The Noverra Group of Companies was founded and is led by three equal Owners, Omar Ladak, Steve Lux and Suzanne Boyd.

Omar Ladak, Owner

Omar is responsible for ensuring strategic alignment between Noverra’s three Owners and our Operating Company Executives. In addition, he works with Steve on the evaluation of new acquisitions to fuel the grown of our group; and with Suzanne on the creation of Leadership Development programing to proactively support the growth of our people. Further he provides mentorship and leadership development support for our most senior Operating Company Executives.

Omar was formerly a Senior Partner and Member of the Board of Directors at RPO Management Consultants, a boutique strategy consultancy with a national Canadian practice which was merged with the Secor Group and ultimately acquired by KPMG Canada.

Steve Lux, Owner

Steve Lux is responsible for the Financial Strategy and Management of the Group as well as Performance Reporting for each of our Operating Companies. In addition, Steve leads the evaluation process for potential new acquisitions, the primary driver of our growth strategy.

Prior to co-founding Noverra, Steve’s experience includes senior advisory roles at RPO Management Consultants and PricewaterhouseCoopers. Steve has almost 20 years of financial and strategic advisory experience having advised clients across Canada, the United States, and internationally on operational, business and financial strategy.
