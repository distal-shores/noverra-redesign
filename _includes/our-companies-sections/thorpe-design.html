Thorpe Design Ltd. is the largest residential fire projection company operating in Northern California. Over 25 years it was grown by Founder Jim Thorpe to be one of the most successful businesses in its industry with over 150 staff operating across five divisions:

  • Tract Development Construction
  • Multi-Family Residential Construction
  • Custom Home Construction
  • Commercial and Industrial Construction
  • Service and Maintenance

Noverra partnered with the Company’s Vice President to acquire the Company and facilitate the owner / CEO Jim Thorpe’s retirement, and supported the Vice President in taking on the responsibilities of President over a transition period. The leadership team at Thorpe has remained unchanged since Noverra’s acquisition of Thorpe and we have proactively focused on developing the existing team to continue Thorpe’s success. Since Noverra acquired the company in 2015, Thorpe has continued to enjoy strong results including the best year in the firm’s 25+ year history.
